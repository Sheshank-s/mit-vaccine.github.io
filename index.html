Conference Topics

Disparities in equitable distribution, vaccine efficacy, duration of immunity, two-dose adherence, and privacy-focused user experience are among the most critical difficulties that must be addressed. Trust at all levels and among all participants is mandatory for success. Can participatory real-time epidemiology and user-centric crowd-sourced solutions help?


This conference will lay the foundation for continued collaboration among all stakeholders to advance basic knowledge and accelerate the practical application of that knowledge to solutions that save lives and reduce human suffering.
