Karuna - A community solution to ensure reliable resource allocation in the fight against Covid19

To fight against the recent outbreak of covid19 cases and the lack of resources in India, we are launching a decentralized web app, Karuna, for crowdsourcing information and providing real time updates of beds, oxygen and hospital supplies in India. Citizens of each city will have access to real-time information about resources close to them, as well as a communication platforms to reach out to doctors and hospitals for a streamlined approach to resource allocation. Surveys integrated in the app can be filled optionally by patients and hospitals to have an easy to use and reliable information database where people take a stand in helping each other. This is a volunteer effort powered by the PathCheck foundation.

For the hospitals and the general population who want a decentralized platform to know where resources are allocated, to reach out to other hospitals and administration and to share information about hospital beds, oxygen, food and other resources, the PathCheck India Karuna system is a web platform that will allow for constructive interaction between hospital administration, doctors and the population while remaining easy to use for all parties. Crowdsourcing using integrated surveys, trackers with real-time positioning of hospital resources available and communications channels will be provided for a reliable and accurate optimization of resource allocation.

Hospitals, doctors and the general population come together in our Karuna app in a community effort to collaborate on:

  1. Crowdsourcing patient recovery and treatment plan in a privacy-preserving manner for covid19 recovered patients.
  2. Have access to hospitals' integrated oxygen and inventory mapping
  3. Early detection and Risk stratification
  4. Variant tracker and super spreader tracking of the new variant
  5. Ending Misinformation about Covid19



Features, Crowdsourcing and More

  1. Surveys: Secure, Anonymous and Privacy-centered crowdsourcing options to help share important information about resources and get accurate predictions of covid19 cases spikes in the future.

  2. Resources: Easily get access to inventory such as beds, oxygen, plasma and more from hospitals near you with a mapped interface of availabilities next to you at all time.

  3. Communication and support: Get access to real time communication channels with hospitals, health officials and covid survivor forces to get verified leads on the resources and help you need in real-time.
